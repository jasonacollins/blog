I have updated my [economics and evolutionary biology reading list](economics_and_evolutionary_biology_reading_list.md), with a few new additions including John Coates's [The Hour Between Dog and Wolf](the-biology-of-boom-and-bust.md), Gregory Clark's [new book on social mobility](the-genetic-basis-of-social-mobility.md) and Jonathan Haidt's [The Righteous Mind](haidts-the-righteous-mind.md). As before, I have been selective, adding only the best books (or articles) in the area. That said, I am always open for suggestions or comment.

When updating the list, I realised I have written a lot of book reviews over the last few years. I have collected most of them together on one page, which you can find [here](posts/book-reviews.md). It includes a lot of good books that aren't on the reading list as they are not on topic. It also contains a few books that are on topic but haven't made the cut.
